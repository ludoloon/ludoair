A buddy of mine recommended taking a flight around the Potato River in Wisconsin. I took off from Spud River Landing Strip. First I went north to get a good view of Lake Superior then turned south to follow the river into the farm lands below.

I'm sure the beauty of the river itself doesn't come across quite like real life, but hey just a good excuse to see it!

I used this as testing grounds for capturing better screenshots so you might notice these are better quality too.
