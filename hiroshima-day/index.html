These photos were captured as part of a series to get footage for some new music videos. So far I haven't made any new music videos, but the footage made for some great photos!

Looking around the map for inspiration, I saw Hiroshima. We learn about how the atomic bomb was dropped there, so naturally I was curious to see what the city "actually" looks like today (virtually anyway). Plus I wanted to get a sense of scale for how big the city is.

I took off from the Iwakuni air base just a few miles south of the city itself. The whole trip was quite beautiful... and terrifying.
